About Our Center

The Center for Agriculture and Climate Change is a leading research institution dedicated to understanding and addressing the complex challenges facing modern agriculture in our changing climate.

Our multidisciplinary team of scientists, agronomists, and climate experts work collaboratively to develop innovative solutions that help farmers adapt to climate variability while maintaining sustainable and productive farming systems.

Through cutting-edge research, community engagement, and policy advocacy, we strive to build a resilient food system that can feed the world while protecting our planet's natural resources.
